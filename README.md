# 💖 Cute Social Tracker 💖

Welcome to the **Cute Social Tracker**! A cozy, adorable, and fully customizable Dalamud plugin for Final Fantasy XIV that helps you keep track of all your sweet social interactions. 

Never miss a `/dote` or a `/pet` again! This plugin quietly listens to the game and logs whenever someone uses your favorite emotes on you, or even when someone is just looking your way. 

## ✨ Features

* **🎀 Adorable Pink UI:** A custom, fully-styled pink interface that replaces the boring default gray windows.
* **👀 Target Tracking:** Wondering who is looking at your glam? The Target tab logs the names and timestamps of anyone who targets your character.
* **💌 Emote History:** Keeps a running log of sweet moments! See exactly who used which emote on you and when.
* **🔍 Custom Emote Filters:** Use the built-in search to track *any* emote in the entire game. Want to track `/hug` or `/slap`? Just add it to your custom list!
* **💬 Chat Notifications:** Optional toggles to print a private echo message directly to your chat box whenever someone targets you or uses a tracked emote.
* **💾 Auto-Saving:** Your history and tracked emotes are automatically saved, so your memories persist even after you close the game!

## 🎮 How to Use

1. Open the game and type `/cuteui` in your chat to open the main tracker window.
2. Navigate to the **Settings** tab to add the specific emotes you want to keep an eye out for.
3. Stand in Limsa (or your favorite bustling city) and watch the history roll in!

## 🛠️ Installation (Manual)

*Since this plugin is currently in development, you can load it manually using XIVLauncher:*

1. Download the latest compiled `.dll` from the Releases tab (or build it yourself!).
2. Open FFXIV and type `/xlsettings` in the chat.
3. Go to the **Experimental** tab.
4. Under "Dev Plugin Locations", add the folder path to where you saved the plugin.
5. Type `/xlplugins` in chat, navigate to **Dev Tools** -> **Installed Dev Plugins**, and enable **Cute Social Tracker**.
