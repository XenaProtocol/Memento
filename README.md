# 💖 Memento 💖

Welcome to **Memento**! A cozy, adorable, and fully customizable Dalamud plugin for Final Fantasy XIV that helps you keep track of all your sweet social interactions.

Never miss a `/dote` or a `/pet` again! This plugin quietly listens to the game and logs whenever someone uses your favorite emotes on you, or even when someone is just looking your way.

## ✨ Features

* 🎀 **Adorable Pink UI:** A custom, fully-styled pink interface featuring beautiful zebra-striped tables that completely replaces the boring default gray windows.
* 📊 **Fun Stats & Paparazzi:** A dedicated trophy room that tracks your lifetime interactions! See who your biggest fans are, which emotes you receive the most, and your total lifetime admirations.
* 👀 **Target Tracking:** Wondering who is looking at your glam? The Target tab logs the names and timestamps of anyone who targets your character.
* 💌 **Emote History:** Keeps a running log of sweet moments! See exactly who used which emote on you and when.
* 🔍 **Custom Emote Filters:** Use the built-in searchable dropdown to track *any* emote in the entire game. Want to track `/hug` or `/slap`? Just add it to your custom list!
* 💬 **Chat Notifications:** Optional toggles to print a private echo message directly to your chat box whenever someone targets you or uses a tracked emote.
* 💾 **Auto-Saving:** Your history, stats, and tracked emotes are automatically saved, so your memories (and high scores!) persist even after you close the game!

## 🎮 How to Use

1. Open the game and type `/memento` in your chat to open the main tracker window.
2. Navigate to the **Settings** tab to add the specific emotes you want to keep an eye out for.
3. Check out the **Stats** tab to see your lifetime totals.
4. Stand in Limsa (or your favorite bustling city) and watch the history roll in!

## 🛠️ Installation

*Since this plugin is currently in development, you can easily install Memento by adding my custom repository to your Dalamud settings!*

1. Open FFXIV and type `/xlsettings` in the chat.
2. Go to the **Experimental** tab.
3. Scroll down to **Custom Plugin Repositories**.
4. Paste this link into an empty box: `https://raw.githubusercontent.com/XenaProtocol/XenaPlugins/refs/heads/main/pluginmaster.json`
5. Click the **+** button and make sure the "Enable" box is checked.
6. Click **Save and Close** at the bottom.
7. Open `/xlplugins`, search for **Memento**, and click Install!
